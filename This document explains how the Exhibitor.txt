This document explains how the Exhibitor Booking Flow of the Exhibition Management System will work — in simple terms. It focuses on what features will be delivered, what each part does, and how the process will work for exhibitors and organizers. 
System Overview
The platform serves as an all-in-one tool for exhibitors to register, book spaces, manage participation, access services, and communicate with organizers, supporting both local and international exhibitions.
Additional Requirement 
Core System Architecture
Frontend Components
    • Public Landing Page: Displays active and upcoming exhibitions with event highlights, schedules, and registration links.
    • Exhibitor Registration & Login Portal: Allows exhibitors to create accounts, log in securely, and access personalized dashboards.
Additional Requirements: Login with OTP and Login with email and password
    • Booking Management Dashboard: Central place for users to book, modify, or cancel spaces, and view booking statuses.
    • Payment Processing Interface: Facilitates secure, multi-method payments with receipt and invoice management.
    • Document Management System: Upload, download, verify, and track important documents in various formats, including certificates and booth designs.
    • Communication Center: Includes messaging, notifications, and  internal inbox for  support for streamlined communication between exhibitors and organizers.
Backend Components
    • User Authentication & Authorization: Ensures secure login and data protection, supporting multiple access levels (Admin, Exhibitor, Staff).
    • Booking Management Engine: Manages bookings, space allocation, pricing, and booking statuses.
    • Payment Gateway Integration: Links with major payment platforms for processing and tracking transactions.
    • Email Notification System: Sends automated emails regarding confirmations, reminders, and updates.
    • Document Storage & Management: Organizes documents across events, ensuring secure storage and version control.
    • Reporting & Analytics Dashboard: Provides insights into booking trends, financial metrics, and user activity.

Detailed Functionality Breakdown

How it works:
    1. Exhibitor visits the public website and explores upcoming exhibitions.
    2. They select the event they want to participate in.
    3. They view the floorplan and choose a booth location.
    4. They complete a booking form with company details and add-on services.
    5. They make payment securely online.
    6. Once confirmed, they upload required documents for verification.
    7. After approval, they can download their exhibitor badges.
    8. On event day, badges are scanned for easy check-in and access control.
